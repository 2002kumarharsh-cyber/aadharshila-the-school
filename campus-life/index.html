Campus Life Overview

A Vibrant Student Ecosystem

At Aadharshila, we believe student development thrives outside the confines of the classroom. We provide room for physical sports, creative arts, and democratic leadership councils.

Our student body is divided into four distinct houses: Vayu (Yellow), Agni (Red), Jal (Blue), and Prithvi (Green). These divisions compete in annual sports tournaments, debates, and art exhibitions, building teamwork and healthy school spirit.
