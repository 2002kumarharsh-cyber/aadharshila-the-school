Preparatory & Primary Wing (Grades 1-5)

Fostering Conceptual foundations

Our Primary wing (Grades 1 to 5) focuses on helping children gain basic literacy, mathematical logic, and environmental curiosity. In this stage, children are introduced to formal textbooks, but the pedagogy remains activity-based.

We emphasize strong reading skills via our structured "Reading Programme" in the central library. We integrate basic coding logic, environment field trips, and hands-on math labs to ensure learners understand theoretical rules in actual operations.

Subjects offered include: English Language & Literature, Hindi, Mathematics, Environmental Studies (EVS), Computer Coding Literacy, and Visual Arts.

Mathematics Lab Kit

Using Jigsaw modules, bead arrays, and geometric shapes to master fractions, decimals, and basic measurements.

Nature trail observations, recycling programs, and leaf printing projects to sensitize pupils to green parameters.

Structured story hour sessions in both English and Hindi to build phonetic confidence and expression skills.
