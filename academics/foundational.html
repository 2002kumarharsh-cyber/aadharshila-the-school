Foundational Stage (Nursery & KG)

Early Years Room
Foundational Playroom

Early childhood Play-way models

Our Foundational program covers Nursery, Lower Kindergarten, and Upper Kindergarten (Ages 3 to 6). We employ progressive play-based methodologies that align with the Foundational Stage guidelines of NEP 2020.

We avoid early text-book load, choosing instead to build gross motor control, alphabet recognition, number sequences, and communication skills through tactile objects, sensory sand zones, story circles, and puppet plays.

Every child-room is air-conditioned, carpeted, and features specialized, rounded furniture units. Our preschool teachers are assisted by dedicated support caretakers to maintain safety limits.

Key Learning Focus Areas

Physical & Motor Skills

Pincer grip building, stacking blocks, clay moulding, safety run pathways, and coordination games.

Cognitive & Logical Skills

Pattern match puzzles, color sorting blocks, simple arithmetic counters, and basic computer graphics clicks.

Language & Art Skills

Phonics drills, rhyme recitals, role plays, paint blending sessions, and group singalongs.
