Middle School Wing (Grades 6-8)

STEM Laboratory
Robotic programming laboratory

Inquiry-Based Learning & Specializations

Our Middle wing (Grades 6 to 8) represents a transition phase from general studies to structured discipline modules. Science splits into Physics, Chemistry, and Biology, while Social Sciences divide into History, Civics, and Geography.

We prioritize STEM skillsets by scheduling mandatory robotics model sessions, scratch coding programs, and basic electronics assemblies. Learners also pick a third language to expand their cultural perspective.

Languages: English, Hindi, Sanskrit, or French. Other core blocks: Algebra/Geometry, Integrated Sciences, Social Sciences, Computer AI, and Physical Training.

Highlighted Middle Wing Assets

Robotics & IoT Block

Coding automated motors, programming sensor chips, and structural assembly designs in weekly STEM hours.

Subject Labs Experiments

Safe chemical reactions, botanical specimen observations under microscopes, and electrical current setups.

Language & Debate Guilds

Structured public speaking forums, spelling contest preparations, and theatrical performance mock drills.
