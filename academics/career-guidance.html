University Placements & Counselling Cell

Guiding Careers, Shaping Futures

Our dedicated **Career Guidance and Counselling Cell** works closely with high schoolers in Grades 10 to 12. We support students with career aptitude profiles, mock tests, and university admissions portfolios.

We host regular workshops for JEE (Engineering), NEET (Medicine), CLAT (Law), and SAT (Global Universities) preparation. Our students receive advice on portfolio building, essay editing, and scholarship registration.

Over the years, our graduates have secured placements at top Indian universities (IITs, NITs, SRCC, St. Stephen's, Ashoka) and international institutions.
