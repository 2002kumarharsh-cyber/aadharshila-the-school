Secondary & Senior Secondary Wing (Grades 9-12)

CBSE Board Preparation & Stream Selections

Our High school wing (Grades 9 to 12) targets academic depth, analytical specialization, and thorough training for the CBSE Board examinations. We maintain advanced science, computer, and arts centers to allow realistic experiment opportunities.

1. Science Stream

Fields: PCM / PCB / PCMC

Advanced Physics, Organic/Inorganic Chemistry, Pure Mathematics, Biology, and Computer Science (Python/SQL). Targets engineering and medical careers.

2. Commerce Stream

Fields: Accounts & Econ

Financial Accountancy, Business Studies, Micro/Macro Economics, and Applied Mathematics. Emphasizes finance, commerce, and corporate audits.

3. Humanities Stream

Fields: Arts & Psychology

Modern History, Political Science & Constitution, General Psychology, English Core, and Geography. Focuses on law, media, and civil services.

Board Performance Preparation Model

Aadharshila features a rigorous evaluation framework for Senior Secondary grades, including weekly test arrays, mid-term examinations, and two cycles of pre-board mock exams marked strictly on CBSE parameters. Mentors provide detailed doubt clarification hours and feedback meetings.
