Curriculum Framework & NEP 2020

Preparing Mindsets for the 2026 Paradigm

At Aadharshila, we structure learning parameters in direct alignment with the National Education Policy (NEP 2020). We move away from rote learning models to emphasize conceptual integration, experiential labs, and active student question prompts.

Our K-12 curriculum follows a structured pedagogical format designed to match developmental milestones from early sensory explorations to high school board specializations.

We leverage digital smart displays, visual model tools, and robotics modules to bring scientific theory to life, while balancing standard humanities and coding programs to ensure holistic growth.
