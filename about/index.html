Our Story & Heritage

A Heritage of Purpose-Driven Education

Established in 2013 by the Chaudhary Bishan Singh Karanwal Foundation near Chandpur in a peaceful, nature-aligned environment, Aadharshila 'The School' is a premier Co-Educational Day School affiliated with CBSE. Now a thriving institution of 2650 students, the school is celebrating the completion of thirteen successful years of hard work and achievement. Our website not only reflects this success story but also stands as a testament to the power of modern online communication.

Aadharshila is widely recognized throughout the academic world for its progressive approach and commitment to excellence, having established its name and fame in the region. The school stands among the best in the district in any field, be it Academics, Co-curricular activities, or Sports. Our students are highly intellectual and smart, while possessing traditional Indian values. The school is known for its high standards of academic performance and the all-round development of every student.

We are blessed to have a highly qualified and talented faculty with the right skills and a positive attitude. Aligned with the school’s motto, “Hand in Hand, We Learn,” our committed staff not only imparts value-based education but also enriches minds and experiences to last a lifetime.
