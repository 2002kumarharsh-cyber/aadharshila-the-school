How to Apply for Session 2026-27

Welcome to Aadharshila. We hope to make our admissions process simple and supportive for parents. Follow the steps below:

Step 1

Online Registration

Submit the Online Admissions Enquiry Form or register at our campus counter. Pay the registration charges (₹1,000).

Step 2

Campus Tour & Interaction

Schedule a visit to see our facilities. Meet with our admissions panel and primary wings coordinators.

Step 3

Student Assessment

A basic conceptual interaction for primary grades, or a syllabus aptitude review (Math/Science/English) for middle & secondary stages.

Step 4

Document Verification

Submit necessary papers including Birth Certificate, Transfer Certificate, last report card, and passport-size photographs.

Step 5

Fee Deposit

Pay admission charges and quarterly dues upon selection to finalize the enrollment slot.
