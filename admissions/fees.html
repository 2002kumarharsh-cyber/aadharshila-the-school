Fee Structure & Policies

Structured Educational Fee Schedule

Fees are determined annually by the Shastri Educational Trust, keeping rates transparent and compliant with state school acts. Dues are billed on a quarterly schedule.

Concessions & Scholarships

  • Sibling Concession: A tuition concession of 10% is available for younger biological siblings.
  • Merit Scholarship: High schoolers scoring 95%+ in Grade 10 Board Examinations qualify for a 50% waiver on tuition fees in Grade 11.
  • Defense Concession: Sincere concession models are extended to wards of serving Indian armed forces personnel.
